what pots dot com is for albert to choose 2 pots that he'd like for his birthday. for each pot, he needs to choose a pot colour (left) and a pot style (right). when chosen, email agnes your pot+color choices using this handy link

there are a number of styles of bowl you can have. they are thrown by agnes. for some idea of what they might look like, look at some examples

each of these colours is made using a combination of 2 glazes. to see the names of the glaze to ask for, hover over the picture. for some idea of what they might look like, look at some examples
